July 21st, 2019

On July 21st, 2019, over 100 armed men dressed in white entered into Yuen Long MTR station and attacked those inside indiscriminately - the elderly, the children, journalists, lawmakers and the protestors.

At least 45 people were injured during this.

The police did not arrive until after the gangsters left, and they did not make any arrests that night. Later on, pictures surfaced online showing a local politician patting men in white shirts on their back, and telling them that they were doing a good job.
